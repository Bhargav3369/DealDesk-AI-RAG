# AcmeCloud Pricing and Objection Notes

AcmeCloud is a fictional SaaS platform used as a local test document for DealDesk AI.

The Free plan includes one project, 1 GB database storage, 2 GB file storage, email support through community channels, and 10,000 monthly active users. Free projects are intended for prototypes and demos.

The Growth plan costs 29 dollars per month and includes five projects, 20 GB database storage, 100 GB file storage, priority email support, daily backups, and 100,000 monthly active users.

The Enterprise plan has custom pricing and includes SSO, audit logs, dedicated support, custom security review support, private networking, and contractual uptime commitments.

If a customer says AcmeCloud is more expensive than a basic hosting platform, explain that AcmeCloud bundles authentication, managed database, storage, backups, and support into one product. For teams that want fewer operational tasks, the bundled platform can reduce setup and maintenance time.

If a customer only needs static hosting, AcmeCloud may not be the best fit. Recommend a dedicated static hosting provider unless the customer also needs auth, database, storage, or backend workflows.
